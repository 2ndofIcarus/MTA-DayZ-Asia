Main Commands
- F1 - Help Panel
- F2 - Settings Panel
- F6 - Group Panel
- T - Local Chat
- Y - Team Chat
- U - Global Chat
- X - Glue to vehicles
- Double Jump - Secure yourself to helicopters when in proximity.
- Backspace - Release yourself from the helicopter.

Animation Commands
- L - Prone
- , - Sit
- . - Raise Hands
- ; - Shout "Friendly"

Console Commands
- /give playerName Amount (gives player money) - Example /give MischiefPlayer2 900
- /kill - Take suicide or Kill yourself.
- /report - Report Bugs/Players/Suggestions.
- /grab - Secure yourself to helicopters when in proximity.
- /drop - Release yourself from the helicopter.
